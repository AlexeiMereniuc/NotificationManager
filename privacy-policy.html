Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 21, 2026

Notification Manager ("the App") is developed by Alexei Mereniuc. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your data.

Data Collection

The App does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data with third parties. All data processed by the App remains exclusively on your device.

Data Stored Locally

To provide its core functionality, the App stores the following information in a local database on your device:

  • Notification content (title, text, app package name, timestamp, channel ID)
  • User-defined filter rules and settings

This data is never sent to any server or external service.

Permissions

The App requires certain Android permissions to function:

  • Notification Access — to read and manage incoming notifications
  • Post Notifications — to repost modified notifications and show service alerts
  • Schedule Exact Alarms — to support alarm snooze functionality
  • Battery Optimization Exemption — to keep the notification listener service running reliably

These permissions are used solely for the App's on-device functionality.

Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Data Retention and Deletion

You have full control over your data. You can delete notification history at any time from within the App. You can also configure automatic cleanup to remove old notifications after a chosen period. Uninstalling the App removes all stored data from your device.

Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. It does not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date.
